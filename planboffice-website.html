Our Story

Refinement.
Curated.

plan b has earned its reputation of embodying refinement, quality, and excellence — offering exceptional furnishings to a discerning clientele. We specialize in contract projects, working exclusively with the world's most innovative design houses.

Based at 380 King St E in Toronto's design district, our showroom is an immersive experience — curated vignettes showcasing each brand in its most considered form.
